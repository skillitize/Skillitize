Course Overview

The Power Platform program equips you with the skills to build enterprise-grade business applications using Microsoft's low-code ecosystem. You will learn to create canvas and model-driven apps with PowerApps, automate business processes with Power Automate, build insightful reports with Power BI, and integrate everything with SharePoint and Dataverse. The course also covers AI Builder and Copilot Studio for adding intelligence to your solutions. Each module is project-based — you will build real applications that solve actual business problems, from approval workflows to document management systems. Power Platform skills are in high demand as organizations seek citizen developers who can bridge the gap between business needs and technology solutions.

What You'll Learn

PowerApps Development

Design and build canvas apps and model-driven apps with custom screens, galleries, forms, and business logic formulas.

Power Automate Flows

Create cloud flows with triggers, actions, conditions, approvals, and connectors to automate repetitive business tasks.

SharePoint Integration

Build custom forms, manage document libraries, create list-driven apps, and integrate SharePoint with the Power Platform ecosystem.

AI Builder & Copilot

Add intelligence to your apps with AI Builder models for form processing, object detection, and text classification, plus Copilot Studio for conversational AI.

Detailed Syllabus

Module 1: Power Platform Overview

  • Power Platform ecosystem: PowerApps, Power Automate, Power BI, Power Pages
  • Licensing models: per-app, per-user, and premium connectors
  • Dataverse: tables, columns, relationships, and data types
  • Environments: development, testing, and production setup
  • Security roles, business units, and data access permissions
  • Solutions framework for application lifecycle management

Module 2: PowerApps Canvas Apps

  • App design: screens, navigation, and responsive layouts
  • Controls: galleries, forms, data tables, charts, and media
  • Formulas: Filter, LookUp, Patch, Collect, UpdateContext, Set
  • Galleries: sorting, searching, filtering, and dynamic templates
  • Forms: edit forms, display forms, validation, and submission logic
  • Variables: context variables, global variables, and collections

Module 3: PowerApps Model-Driven Apps

  • Dataverse table design: columns, data types, and relationships
  • Views: system views, personal views, and advanced filtering
  • Business rules for field validation and conditional logic
  • Dashboards: interactive charts, lists, and embedded Power BI
  • Forms customization: tabs, sections, and sub-grids
  • Business process flows for guided user experiences

Module 4: Power Automate

  • Cloud flows: automated, instant, and scheduled triggers
  • Triggers: when an item is created, modified, on a schedule, or on-demand
  • Actions: conditions, loops, compose, variables, and expressions
  • Approval flows: single-stage, multi-stage, and parallel approvals
  • Connectors: Outlook, Teams, SharePoint, SQL, HTTP, and custom connectors
  • Error handling: try-catch patterns, run-after configuration, and retry policies

Module 5: Power BI Integration

  • Connecting Power BI to Dataverse, SharePoint, SQL, and Excel sources
  • Building reports: visuals, filters, slicers, and drill-through pages
  • Embedding Power BI reports inside PowerApps and SharePoint
  • Row-level security and data access governance
  • Scheduled refresh and data gateway configuration
  • Creating actionable dashboards for business stakeholders

Module 6: SharePoint Integration

  • SharePoint lists: columns, views, and content types
  • Document libraries: metadata, versioning, and check-in/check-out
  • Custom PowerApps forms for SharePoint lists
  • Document management workflows with Power Automate
  • SharePoint site design and information architecture
  • Permissions, sharing, and external access management

Module 7: AI Builder & Copilot

  • AI Builder overview: prebuilt models vs. custom models
  • Form processing: extracting data from invoices and receipts
  • Object detection: training models to identify items in images
  • Text classification: categorizing emails, feedback, and support tickets
  • Copilot Studio: building conversational AI bots without code
  • Integrating AI models into PowerApps and Power Automate

Module 8: Capstone Project

  • Business process automation app: requirements gathering and design
  • PowerApps frontend: canvas app with data entry forms and dashboards
  • Power Automate backend: approval workflows and notifications
  • SharePoint integration: document storage and list management
  • Power BI reporting: embedded analytics and KPI tracking
  • Deployment, testing, user acceptance, and documentation

Who Is This For?

  • Business professionals who want to build apps and automate workflows without traditional coding
  • IT administrators and SharePoint admins looking to expand into Power Platform development
  • Project managers and team leads who want to digitize manual business processes
  • Fresh graduates interested in the growing low-code/no-code development market
  • Developers who want to add Microsoft Power Platform to their consulting toolkit
